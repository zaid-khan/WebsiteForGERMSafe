MADE IN INDIA

WE ARE

GermSAFE Technology LLP, incorporated in the year 2019, we provide clean solution for water treatment with the goal of zero water discharge from industry, using unique and innovative technology developed in-house. We work primarily on developing clean and eco-friendly solutions for drinking and waste water treatment. We are awarded and recognized by various start-up initiative projects funded by IOCL, Infosys, Department of Biotechnology etc. GermSAFE Technology is unique in the water treatment area because of say No to chemicals AGENDA.

WE DO

In India, Government has started the SWACHA BHARAT MISSION which is focusing on cleanliness issues related to water, air and land pollution. To address the government mission and understanding the demand of zero discharge in chemical industries, hotels, airports etc. we need sustainable and innovative development. We serve clean, innovative and sustainable solutions for the water treatment which makes GermSAFE Technology a unique company.

WHERE WE DO

GermSAFE Technology operate has registered office in Pune and is also working in Mumbai. The team is made up of highly qualified scientist from renowned Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai.
