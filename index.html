MADE IN INDIA

WE ARE

GermSAFE Technology a Limited Liability Partnership company, incorporated in the year 2019, we provide clean solution for water treatment with the goal of zero water discharge from industry, using unique and innovative technology developed in-house. We work primarily on developing clean and eco-friendly solutions for drinking and waste water treatment. We are awarded and recognized by various start-up initiative projects funded by Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Infosys foundation, Department of Biotechnology, Department of Science and Technology etc. GermSAFE Technology is unique in the water treatment area because of say “NO TO CHEMICALS AGENDA”.

WE DO

In India, Government has started the “SWACHA BHARAT MISSION” which is mainly focusing on cleanliness issues related to water, air and land pollution. To address the government mission and understanding the demand of zero discharge in chemical industries, hotels, airports etc. we need sustainable and innovative development. We serve clean, innovative and sustainable solutions for the water treatment which makes GermSAFE Technology a unique company.

WHERE WE DO

GermSAFE Technology has registered office in Pune and is also working in Mumbai. The team is made up of highly qualified research scholars from renowned Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai.
